UFOs are real. No doubt about it. I would know.

Written by Luke Groundwalker

I never expected it to happen to me. One day, I was minding my own business, and the next day, I was changed by aliens from the sky. I was terrified, and I had no idea what was happening. The next thing I knew, I was being subjected to some sort of brain surgery. As unbelievable as it sounds, this is my story: I was abducted by a UFO and had brain surgery.


The day it happened

I was just minding my own business, walking down the street on a sunny day. The birds were chirping, but there was a dark shape over me, humming a weird tune. Suddenly, I felt a strange force pulling me up off the ground, almost like a strong gust of wind. I looked around and realized that I was being sucked up into the air and toward a UFO. I was so stunned that I didn't even have time to scream or react in any way. In what felt like only seconds, I was inside the UFO, surrounded by strange aliens. Before I could even get my bearings, I was taken to another room where they began performing some type of brain surgery on me.


Inside the UFO

The moment I stepped foot inside the UFO, I was filled with a sense of awe and terror. The walls were lined with mysterious lights that illuminated the chamber, and there was a strange hum in the air. In the center of the room stood three tall figures cloaked in white robes, their faces obscured by blue masks.

As they approached me, I could make out some faint words in a language I'd never heard before. They seemed to be talking amongst themselves, occasionally uttering the word "Oops" as if they were worried about something. All of a sudden, they began to move quickly around me, almost as if they were operating. As I watched in horror, one of them pulled out a device and placed it on my forehead. I heard them mutter something like "He'll be fine," and then everything went black.


Waking up

I awoke to find myself in a strange operating room, surrounded by beings with advanced technology I had never seen before. They were carefully removing me from the bed and taking me over to the operating table. I was paralyzed with fear as I watched them place some kind of device on my head and administer a sedative.

The next thing I knew, I felt intense pain as the aliens operated on my brain. I could feel them drilling and prodding around inside my skull, and it felt like my head was about to explode. Despite my attempts to scream for help, I was unable to move or make a sound. The only thing I could do was lie there and endure the pain until it finally stopped.

When the operation was over, I felt drained and exhausted. The aliens somehow managed to manipulate my brain, leaving me feeling disoriented and confused. I had no idea what they had done to me or why, but I knew that something had changed inside me forever.
