Microwaves: Brain Fryers

Written by Brian Flyers

  You walk into your kitchen, and what do you see? A microwave, of course! And why do you see a microwave in your kitchen? It doesn’t look good, it doesn’t make food taste good, and it gets smelly. Well, I have found out the point of the microwave. This seems totally unrealistic, but the truth is that the point of microwaves is to fry your brain. Now, to the average human this may sound crazy. However, according to recent studies, microwaves are found to have a small radioactive microchip in them that will, undoubtedly, fry the majority of your brain cells. Before you go shoot or destroy your microwave, you must understand that only government-issued microwaves have the ability to fry your brain. To check if yours is by the government, go to the documentation of your microwave, and look. These days, nearly all home microwaves are approved by the government though, so you are probably right to kill your microwave.

  Here is a quote from Dr. Donothing, the same brain doctor who operated on my friend Luke Groundwalker, “You know, ...microwaves...can...fry...your... more important...brain...cells...to the point…of ...stupidity...” He said all of those words over the span of eleven years in that exact order.


How does it really work?

  If you are a sciencey sort of person, this next part is for you. I’m going to explain how the microwaves are able to fry our brains. The government implants a microchip (right next to the round thing, and on top of the square thing) in them when they are first sent out of the factories. The chip, now a part of the works of the microwave, is where it all happens. Whenever you press a button anywhere on the microwave, it activates the chip. The chip then sends out a wave of radioactivity that will reach anything within a twelve-foot (3.6576 meter) radius. The radioactivity is a certain kind that only fries/destroys squishy things, e.g. your brain, your pet worm, etc. Anyway, the radioactivity finds its way in through your ear holes, then to your brain. Once in your brain, it burns and fries your brain cells. Now, it’s not very strong, so before you freak out, know that it takes a little more than a thousand waves to fry a very tiny area of your brain.


Why though?

  Here is the part you’ve been waiting for: the ‘why’ of this whole phenomenon. We, after extensive research and surveillance, have discovered the government’s reason for trying to fry your brain. This whole time, it has been for secrecy. You see, the government is trying to make us citizens even dumber than we already are. By frying our brain cells, they have begun to make us less observant of our surroundings. That way, we won’t notice the aliens, Bigfoot, alien abductions, etc. That’s right! The government is hiding many things behind closed doors.


What to do next

  So, now you’re wundering about what you can do about this evil microwave in your house. Well, there are a few things you can do. There are a few kinds of people that come from reading this article:

  • The guy who utterly destroys his microwave and then frantically searches online for a non-government-approved microwave to buy instead.
  • The person who keeps giving their microwave suspicious glares.
  • That one guy who throws it into his garage and forgets about the microwave.
  • The person who neatly disposes of his microwave and then never gets another microwave. EVER.
